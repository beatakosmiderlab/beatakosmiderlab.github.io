Postdoctoral Fellow Position

Status: Accepting Applications

We are seeking a highly motivated Postdoctoral Fellow to join our research team. The successful candidate will lead projects investigating alveolar type II cell biology, lung injury and repair mechanisms, and emphysema pathogenesis.

Requirements:

  • PhD in molecular biology, cell biology, biochemistry, or related field
  • Strong publication record
  • Experience with cell culture, mouse models, and molecular biology techniques
  • Excellent written and communication skills

How to Apply:

Please send your CV, cover letter, and contact information for three references to beata.kosmider@temple.edu
